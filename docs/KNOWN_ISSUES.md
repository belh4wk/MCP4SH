# Known issues — v1.0

- MCP4SH was tuned primarily on a specific 4-transducer rig. Other hardware layouts may still require meaningful gain adjustments.
- Some games expose weaker, noisier, delayed, or incomplete telemetry. In those titles, scrub / ABS / slip behaviour may feel simpler or rougher than intended.
- GT7 support is not yet where I want it to be.
- The Gearbox / Grinds behaviour is still an area to treat with caution.
- “Out of the box” does not mean “identical on every rig.” Placement, amplifier gain structure, seat construction, and transducer choice still matter.
- Legacy preview files may still exist in the repo for archival reasons. v1.0 is the supported line.
